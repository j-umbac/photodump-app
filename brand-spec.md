# Brand Specification - PhotoDump Premium Native

Extracted from the user's "Vision" document.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF` (True White) or `#F5F5F7` (Off-white surface)
- **Primary Text**: `#000000` (Pure Black)
- **Secondary Text**: `#71717A` (Zinc-500)
- **Accent**: `#0071E3` (Apple Blue)

## Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: San Francisco Pro (SF Pro)
- **Fallback**: Inter
- **Headings**: SF Pro Display (Semibold/Bold, Tight Tracking)
- **Body**: SF Pro Text (Regular, Leading-relaxed)

## Radii
- **Cards/Modals**: `rounded-2xl` (0.75rem) or `rounded-3xl` (1rem)
- **Buttons**: `rounded-full` (Pill) or `rounded-xl` (0.75rem)
- **Shadcn Radius Token**: `1rem`

## Shadow & Depth
- **Primary Shadow**: `0 4px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.04)` (Soft, diffused)
- **Glassmorphism**: `backdrop-blur-xl` + `bg-white/70`

## Motion
- **Easing**: `cubic-bezier(0.32, 0.72, 0, 1)` (Natural friction)
- **Hover**: `scale-[1.02]` on cards
